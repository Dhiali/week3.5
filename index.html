1. Are dogs allowed on the cruise?
Yes, we warmly welcome dogs on our cruises. We're a pet-friendly cruise company, and we love having your furry friends aboard.
2. What accommodations are available for pets?
We offer pet-friendly cabin options where you and your dog can stay comfortably. These cabins are equipped with amenities to ensure a pleasant stay for both you and your pet.
3. Are there designated areas for dogs to play on the ship?
Absolutely! We have designated play areas on the ship where your dog can have fun and socialize with other canine passengers.
4. Can I bring my dog's favorite toys and bedding?
Yes, you are encouraged to bring your dog's favorite toys, bedding, and other familiar items to help them feel at home during the cruise.
5. What is the leash policy on the ship?
For the safety and comfort of all passengers, we require dogs to be leashed or in a carrier when they are in public areas on the ship.
We hope these FAQs provide you with valuable information for your upcoming dog cruise adventure. If you have any more questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our friendly team.
